Cryptocurrency technology is complex and often confusing. It’s important to understand what’s at stake in our project before participating. We’re here to answer your questions and help you get started. Let’s talk!

Will Texas – with its robust economy, independent power grid and unique cultural identity – go down with the ship? Experts at the Texas Nationalist Movement will show exactly how & why Texas Independence is the only plausible future.

How should we know? Only the market can decide what it will use as money for honest trade. Here’s what we do know: first, our team is made of complementary currency experts, and we know exactly how to get an alternative currency trading in the market.

Second, we will use & spend TXC to actively promote our mission to give TEXITcoin a head-start in the marketplace.

TEXITcoin is created exclusively by Bobby Gray, founder of the American Open Currency Standard (2008), creator of the original Bitcoin coin (2011), US Congressional Domestic Monetary Services Subcommittee expert witness (2012), and creator of the original Bitcoin Cold Storage Coin (2017). He lives in Texas with his family.

Purchasing TEXITcoin is NOT an investment. TXC is highly speculative and no one should purchase any more than they are prepared to completely lose. Think of TXC more like gambling or buying a lottery ticket. There’s a chance you can win big, but it’s only a chance.

Unlike other digital collectible offerings like NFT’s, TXC is an actual cryptocurrency, designed to be used as a medium of exchange for those in & around Texas. There is NO pre-mine, unlike tokens that have next to no purpose or functional utility use.

The team behind TXC is highly experienced in monetary policy, economics, and complementary currencies, and we intend to build a network of merchants and business owners who will trade goods and services for TXC.
